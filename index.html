FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before we work together.

What exactly does an AI Marketing Specialist do? ＋
I use advanced AI tools to create professional marketing content — social media posts, brand designs, ad copy, and full marketing strategies — faster and at higher quality than traditional methods. Think of it as having a full marketing team powered by the latest AI technology, at a fraction of the cost.
Do you work with businesses outside the USA? ＋
Absolutely. All my services are 100% remote and digital. I work with clients across the USA, Canada, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and anywhere in the world. If you have an internet connection, we can work together.
How long does it take to deliver content? ＋
Delivery times depend on the package. Typically: Social media content packs are delivered within 3-5 business days. Brand identity projects take 5-7 business days. Rush delivery (24-48 hours) is available for an additional fee. I always confirm timelines before starting any project.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result? ＋
Your satisfaction is my priority. Every package includes up to 2 revision rounds at no extra cost. If after revisions you are still not happy, we will discuss a fair solution together. I believe in long-term relationships, not one-time transactions.
Can you create content in both Arabic and English? ＋
Yes — this is one of my core strengths. Arabic is my native language and English is my professional working language. I don't just translate; I create original content in both languages that feels natural and authentic to each audience.
How do I get started? ＋
It's simple: 1) Request your free sample using the button below. 2) I'll create a sample piece of content for your business at no charge. 3) If you love it, we discuss the right package for your needs. No pressure, no obligation.
What payment methods do you accept? ＋
I accept PayPal, Zelle, bank transfer, and payments through Fiverr (which offers buyer protection). For recurring monthly clients, I also accept Venmo. All transactions are secure and documented.
